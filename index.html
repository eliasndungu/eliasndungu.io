Our Journey

The Road to Innovation

From idea to impactful reality - the Neuracraft story

January 2024

The Beginning

Neuracraft was founded with a vision to transform how agricultural payments are processed in Kenya. Initial prototype developed focusing on milk collection records.

February 2024

First Partnership

Secured our first major client - a dairy cooperative looking to modernize their payment systems for thousands of farmers. Integrated with M-Pesa API for seamless payments.

March 2024

Technical Challenges

Faced significant data inconsistency issues as scale increased. Our team worked around the clock to develop advanced data cleaning algorithms to maintain accuracy.

April 2024

Breakthrough Innovation

Developed proprietary anomaly detection system that identifies "ghost farmers" and fraudulent records with remarkable precision, saving clients thousands in prevented fraud.

Today

Growing Impact

$7.2 million in payments processed with 99.8% accuracy. Expanding beyond agriculture into small business analytics and payment processing solutions across multiple sectors.
