Meet Elias Ndungu

Product builder connecting AI, IoT, and data science to real-world supply chains

Who I Am

I'm Elias Ndungu, creating platforms that make operations feel seamless for farmers, factory teams, and shopkeepers. My work spans computer vision, OCR, AI/ML, and integrations that keep people and data connected.

Mission

Ship products that remove manual friction—digitizing milk journals, reading live machine telemetry, and syncing wholesale deliveries—so teams can focus on growth instead of paperwork.

AI + Data Science

Computer vision, OCR, and predictive models that keep data clean and reliable

IoT & Telematics

Probes and sensors that surface live machine health and energy signals

Platform Integrations

Journey

Launches Across Industries

Milestones from Neuracraft to AutoNeuix to DukaSync

2024

Neuracraft Goes Live

Launched Neuracraft as an end-to-end digital milk platform connecting farmers to milk companies and milk sellers. Requests feel like social connections, making onboarding as easy as adding a friend.

2024

AI + OCR Foundation

Digitized milk journals with OCR, layered AI models to keep records clean, and opened paid annotation jobs so teams can improve datasets while earning.

2025

AutoNeuix Launch

Launched in 2025, streaming real-time telematics from factory machinery using probes and sensors. Applied AI and data science to predict machine health, electrical usage, and uptime.

January 2026

DukaSync Release

Launched DukaSync for wholesalers and shopkeepers, inheriting Neuracraft's core to sync deliveries with zero manual stock entry and an API for existing wholesale software.

Today

Building What's Next

Continuing to connect agriculture, industrial operations, and retail. Open to collaborations that need AI, OCR, sensors, and APIs to remove operational friction.

Ventures

Featured Platforms

Three products connecting agriculture, manufacturing, and retail with AI, OCR, IoT, and APIs

Neuracraft | Milk Supply Chain

End-to-end digital platform connecting farmers to milk companies and milk sellers. AI and OCR digitize milk journals while paid annotation jobs keep datasets improving.

  • AI + OCR milk journal digitization
  • Social-style farmer and company connections
  • Annotation jobs that pay contributors

AutoNeuix | Industrial Telematics

Uses probes and sensors to read live machine telematics, electrical usage, and operating hours. AI and data science predict health to minimize downtime.

  • Real-time probes and sensors
  • Predictive maintenance models
  • Energy and uptime logging

DukaSync | Wholesale to Shopkeepers

Inherits Neuracraft to sync wholesaler deliveries to shopkeepers with no manual stock entry. API-ready for existing wholesale software with two-way transfer support.

  • Seamless stock transfer workflows
  • API integrations with legacy tools
  • Connect wholesalers and shopkeepers
